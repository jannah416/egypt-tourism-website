Giza Pyramids & Sphinx

The visit to Egypt is not completed yet without a tour to Giza Pyramids & Sphinx. Get a glimpse of the greatness of Pharaohs and enjoy a tour through the three great pyramids of Giza in a day trip. It's a guided walk through history among Egypt's greatest attractions. If there're things you should do when visiting Cairo, this Cairo day tour is on top of the list.

TOUR INFORMATION

You’ll start your tour with Travel To Egypt’s tour guide, who’ll pick you up from the hotel to start your day trip. First on the list, the visit to the Pyramids of Cheops, Chephren & Mykerinus. Followed by the 73 meters high Great Sphinx, the guard of this great Egyptian plateau. Next will be entering the valley temple, the place where the great Pharaohs did the famous mummification for their kings
